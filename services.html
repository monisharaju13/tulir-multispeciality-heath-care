Our Services

At TULIR MULTISPECIALITY HEALTH CARE, we offer a comprehensive range of medical services to meet the diverse needs of our patients. Our experienced specialists are dedicated to providing high-quality care in a comfortable and compassionate environment.

Podiatrist

Our podiatry department offers expert care for foot and ankle conditions. From routine check-ups to advanced treatments, we provide comprehensive services to keep your feet healthy and pain-free.

Surgical Oncology

Our surgical oncology team specializes in the surgical treatment of cancer. We offer cutting-edge surgical techniques and compassionate care to ensure the best outcomes for our patients.

Physician and Diabetologist

Our diabetologists offer specialized care for patients with diabetes. We provide personalized treatment plans, education, and support to help you manage your diabetes effectively.

ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat)

Our ENT specialists provide comprehensive care for conditions affecting the ear, nose, and throat. From diagnosis to treatment, we offer a wide range of services to address your ENT needs.

Plastic Surgery

Our plastic surgery department offers a range of cosmetic and reconstructive procedures. Our skilled surgeons are committed to helping you achieve your aesthetic goals and improve your quality of life.
